Start Distributing

Distribute to 40+ Streaming Platforms

Souloryx Records makes it easy for artists to distribute their music globally. To get started, follow these steps:

  1. Prepare Your Music: Ensure your song or album is in a high-quality audio format (e.g., WAV, FLAC, or MP3).
  2. Metadata Submission: Provide detailed information about your release, including track titles, album artwork (minimum 3000x3000 pixels), release date, and genre.
  3. Ownership and Licensing: Confirm that you own the rights to the music or have the appropriate licensing.
  4. Copyright Agreement: By using our service, you automatically agree to Souloryx Records handling your music's copyright.
  5. Submit for Review: Once all details are completed, submit your music for review and distribution. We'll handle delivering your tracks to over 40 streaming platforms, including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, and more.

After submission, you will retain 70% of the revenue, and we take care of everything else, from distribution to copyright management.
